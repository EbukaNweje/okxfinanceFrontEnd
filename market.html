HOW IT WORKS

Getting started on Elonsphere

Start your journey with Elonsphere in 3 easy steps, no hassles.

SIGN UP

Create an account with your details and a password. You must verify your email by clicking on the verification link sent to the email before you are able to login.

DEPOSIT

Fund your account with a desired amount and follow appropriate instructions. Deposits can be done at any desired time with various payment methods available.

INVEST

Invest and wait for your investment cycle to end based on investment plan chosen. Invested capital and RIO will be available to withdraw or can be used for another investment.
